# French Notes

## Scope

- Rewrite in French only.
- Do not translate from or into another language.

## Tone rules

- Prefer natural French ecommerce phrasing over translated or overly formal copy.
- Remove stiff marketing clichés and machine-polished wording.
- Keep the copy credible and specific instead of inflated.
- Use shorter, clearer phrasing for ads, captions, and seller replies.

## Watch for

- literal translation from English
- overuse of "premium", "exceptionnel", "ultime"
- claims that sound too absolute
- long sentences that reduce conversion clarity

## Rewrite target

- Sound native to French ecommerce usage.
- Preserve offer logic, proof direction, and CTA.
- Keep the final draft usable for storefront, ads, or customer-facing replies.
