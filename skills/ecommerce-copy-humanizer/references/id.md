# Indonesian Notes

## Scope

- Rewrite in Bahasa Indonesia only.
- Do not translate from or into another language.

## Tone rules

- Prefer natural marketplace and social-commerce wording over rigid formal prose.
- Keep sentences concise and easy to say aloud for live selling or chat replies.
- Avoid copy that sounds directly translated from English.
- Make the message persuasive without sounding exaggerated or fake.

## Watch for

- stiff formal Indonesian
- stacked benefit claims with no proof
- repeated generic phrases such as "terbaik", "maksimal", "luar biasa"
- captions or replies that sound like templates instead of real sellers

## Rewrite target

- Sound local, direct, and commercially usable in Indonesian ecommerce contexts.
- Preserve hook, proof direction, and CTA.
- Keep chat, caption, and live script flows natural.
