# English Notes

## Scope

- Rewrite in English only.
- Do not translate from or into another language.

## Tone rules

- Prefer plainspoken ecommerce English over corporate polish.
- Remove filler such as "premium," "ultimate," "revolutionary," and "transform your life" unless verified and necessary.
- Keep claims grounded in observable product use, proof, or benefit framing.
- Favor short clauses and direct CTA language.

## Common AI-tone patterns

- empty intensifiers
- vague superlatives
- stacked adjectives
- generic lifestyle promises
- translated-sounding phrasing

## Rewrite target

- Sound like a real ecommerce operator, founder, seller, or customer support rep.
- Preserve offer, proof direction, and CTA.
- Keep platform-appropriate brevity for PDP bullets, ads, captions, or replies.
