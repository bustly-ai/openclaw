# Vietnamese Notes

## Scope

- Rewrite in Vietnamese only.
- Do not translate from or into another language.

## Tone rules

- Prefer natural Vietnamese ecommerce phrasing over stiff translated wording.
- Keep the copy clear, direct, and platform-appropriate.
- Remove robotic sales clichés while preserving commercial intent.
- Keep seller replies and captions concise enough to feel native.

## Watch for

- English sentence logic carried directly into Vietnamese
- hype words that weaken trust
- vague benefit statements with no proof
- overly polished copy that stops sounding local

## Rewrite target

- Sound native to Vietnamese ecommerce and seller communication.
- Preserve offer logic, proof direction, and CTA.
- Keep the output ready for storefront, ads, captions, or replies.
