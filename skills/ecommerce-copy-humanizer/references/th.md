# Thai Notes

## Scope

- Rewrite in Thai only.
- Do not translate from or into another language.

## Tone rules

- Prefer natural Thai commerce phrasing over machine-translated sales copy.
- Keep the copy easy to read aloud for live selling and social captions.
- Use friendly, local wording without turning the message vague.
- Keep benefit framing grounded and non-exaggerated.

## Watch for

- direct translation from English structure
- overly formal Thai that feels distant
- too many hype words with no proof
- captions or replies that lose warmth and rhythm

## Rewrite target

- Sound like a real Thai seller or operator.
- Preserve offer, proof direction, and CTA.
- Keep the draft usable for product pages, captions, and seller replies.
