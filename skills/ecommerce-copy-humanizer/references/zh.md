# Chinese Notes

## Scope

- Rewrite in Chinese only.
- Do not translate from or into another language.

## Tone rules

- Prefer natural ecommerce Chinese over AI clichés and empty big words.
- Keep the copy grounded in product use, proof, and conversion intent.
- Make the sentence rhythm closer to real operators, streamers, or seller replies.
- Keep the final wording concise enough for PDP, comments, captions, and live scripts.

## Watch for

- 套话式大词
- 机器感强的并列形容词
- 没有证据支撑的夸大表述
- 翻译腔或官腔

## Rewrite target

- 像真实商家、主播、运营或客服在说话。
- 保留卖点、证据方向和 CTA。
- 输出可直接用于商品页、评论回复、直播话术或广告文案。
