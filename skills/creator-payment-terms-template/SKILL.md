# Creator Payment Terms Template

## Skill Card

- **Category:** Creator Operations
- **Core problem:** Payment ambiguity causes friction, disputes, and delays in creator campaigns.
- **Best for:** Teams standardizing creator payout expectations before launch.
- **Expected input:** Collaboration model, deliverables, acceptance rules, payment timing, extra terms.
- **Expected output:** Payment structure summary + risk points + copy-ready terms.

4. Convert the diagnosis into P0 / P1 / P2 actions tied to evidence, not generic best practices.
5. End with a validation loop so the operator knows exactly how to recheck if the fix worked.

## Example

**User:** I need payment terms for a creator campaign: 3 videos, $1,500 total, paid after delivery. Draft the terms.

**Assistant:** Payment structure: $1,500 flat fee for 3 deliverables. Milestone: 50% ($750) upon first draft approval, 50% ($750) within 7 business days of final delivery acceptance. Risk points: "paid after delivery" is vague -- specify acceptance criteria and payment window. Copy-ready terms and creator-facing explanation provided. Recommendation: add a 2-revision cap per video to protect both sides.
