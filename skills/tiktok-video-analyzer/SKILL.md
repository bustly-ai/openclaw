# TikTok Video Analyzer

Analyze a TikTok video at a lightweight, practical level using the information available: title, screenshots, visible structure, metrics, comments, and optional captions or transcript.

Use this skill when a user wants a fast, useful breakdown of why a TikTok video may be working, what can be learned from it, and which parts are worth adapting for ecommerce, creator content, or short-form strategy.

## Problem it solves
People often see a strong TikTok video but cannot explain why it works. They can describe the vibe, but not the structure, hooks, visual pulls, or commerce lessons. This skill provides a lightweight analysis without pretending to do full video-model understanding when only partial inputs are available.

## What this skill is for
This is a lightweight analyzer, not a full video intelligence engine.
It works best when the user can provide some combination of:
- Video title
- Screenshots / key frames
- View, like, comment, and engagement data
- Comments
- Visible hooks or on-screen text
- Optional captions / transcript

It is especially useful for:
- “Why did this video work?”
- “What can I copy from this structure?”
- “What parts are worth applying to ecommerce or TikTok Shop?”
- “How do I turn this video into a social post or content brief?”

## Use when
- A user provides a TikTok link plus screenshots/data
- A team wants a quick teardown of a winning or losing video
- The goal is learning, adaptation, repurposing, or content strategy
- The user wants to extract content lessons from a video case
